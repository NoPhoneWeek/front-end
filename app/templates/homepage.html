#DownWeek

Sign Up

August 8 — 12

Monday — Friday

Spend one week this summer away from technology.

DownWeek is a week-long event for high school- and college-aged people to come together away from their phones. Sign up to connect with those around you:

  • Elect a neighborhood meetup place to hang out with other DownWeek participants
  • Propose and attend DownWeek events in your neighborhood
  • Connect with Facebook friends and invite them to meetups and events
  • Arrange your week in advance. DownWeek.com will close on July 31 at 11:59:59 PM and reopen once the week is over

Share

Help power the cultural revolution.

Invite your three best friends right now and you'll without question have an incredible week. No matter what else happens. Just send them a message with the link "DownWeek.com."

Have them invite their three best friends, and you'll have a movement.


Guidelines

It's up to you

No matter what, you may not use your phone, computer, or TV, or the internet.

  • Allowing yourself to drive during DownWeek is optional
  • Lights and all other electricity are optional
  • If you have a job or other commitment during DownWeek, observe as much of it as you can
